The Southbank

This season, Wintertime at Southbank Centre is pulling out all the stops to throw its biggest and best ever Christmas celebration (running from Friday November 10 2017 to Thursday 4 January 2018). There's a sack-load of Thames-side festive fun planned including one of the best Christmas markets in London, where shoppers can bag gifts, festive produce and sweet treats, and seasonal Christmas shows, guaranteed to brighten up any miserable family members shunning the festive spirit. Check out our highlights below and head down to one of the most festive events in London this winter.

Closest tube: Waterloo

Dates: 10th November 2017 - 4th January 2018

Winter Wonderland

For over 10 years, Hyde Park’s Winter Wonderland has been spreading the Christmas spirit throughout London.The winter festival has become a landmark event for Londoners and tourists alike. With humble beginnings as an open-air Christmas market in Hyde Park, Winter Wonderland has since grown into an extravaganza with ice skating, shows, roller coaster rides, street food stalls, festive bars and live music. It is still free to enter the Winter Wonderland grounds and take in the joyous atmosphere. With over 100 spectacular rides and attractions, it is no wonder why thousands of visitors return to Winter Wonderland each year to celebrate the Christmas season. For 2017, Winter Wonderland is offering a dazzling array of activities and entertainment. Enjoy skating around the UK’s largest open-air ice rink, have a deep sea adventure in the Magical Ice Kingdom, see thrilling circus shows, world-class ice dancing in Cinderella on Ice and see stunning views across London in the Giant Observation Wheel and much more.

Closest tube: Hyde Park Corner or Marble Arch

Dates: 17th November 2017 - 1st January 2018

Greenwich Market

Greenwich Market is packed full of unique and distinctive arts and crafts. Well known for its designer makers and small cottage industries, customers have access to items that cannot be found anywhere else in the world. Enjoy the vibrant atmosphere, take in some classical music performed by students from nearby Trinity College of Music, and enjoy the continental food court whilst browsing through the wonderful array of over 120 arts, craft and antique stalls. Read more at https://www.visitlondon.com/things-to-do/place/159294-greenwich-market#IqZWVPXLClB4ymg0.99

Closest tube: Greenwich

Dates: All year round

Chelsea Physic Garden

The Chelsea Physic Garden Christmas Fair is a chance for our friends and visitors alike to discover the most stylish and exquisite gift ideas and festive inspiration. Soak up the festive atmosphere whilst selecting unique gifts you won't find on the high street. Stay for a delicious lunch, a glass of something festive and select your own unique handmade Christmas wreath created by our wonderful team of volunteers using seeds and foliage from The Garden. Join us for a performance of carols at the fair on 4pm on Saturday 25 November. What a wonderful way to herald in the season! Children are free, please select the children's ticket option at the checkout.

Festive Fantasia in Croydon

Head to Croydon this festive season to discover quirky light and video installations, a food market and plenty of entertainment. The celebrations begin on 16 November with the Festive Lights Street Feast, in North End from 12pm to 7.30pm. Expect more than 20 street food stalls selling delicacies from around the world, plus live music featuring McGoozer, and southern blues band, the MonoTones.

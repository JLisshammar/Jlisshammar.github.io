Christmas in London
About us
This site is made as part of our coding project to give some tips and hints on some interesting things to do in London for Christmas 2017. This site offers top five tips for events, nightlife and christmas markets. It’s beginning to look a lot like Christmas in London. The sparkling festive lights have been switched on and markets, ice-rinks and villages are popping up all over the capital. That's why we hope you can use this site as a guide to seeing London at it's most beautiful!
